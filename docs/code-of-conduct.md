# Code of Conduct

The Abu Dhabi AI PropTech Challenge is a professional, builder-first event. Everyone — participants, mentors, judges, organizers — is expected to keep it that way.

## The standard

- **Be respectful.** Treat everyone with courtesy regardless of background, experience level, gender, nationality, religion, or anything else.
- **Be collaborative.** Help other teams when asked. Competition is for the demo stage, not the hallway.
- **Be honest.** Present your own work, built during the event. Don't misrepresent what your prototype does.
- **Respect the venue.** Hub71 is hosting us — follow venue rules and staff instructions.
- **Respect the data.** Use the provided synthetic datasets or legitimate public data. No real personal data, no scraping private sources.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, intimidation, or discrimination of any kind
- Unwelcome sexual attention or imagery
- Sustained disruption of talks, demos, or other teams' work
- Plagiarism or submitting pre-built projects as event work
- Photographing or recording people who have asked not to be

## Scope

This applies at the venue, on Discord, in GitHub repos and Issues, and in any other event channel — for the full duration of the event and follow-up.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness a violation, contact any organizer at the venue or message the organizer team on Discord (https://discord.gg/jy3QDxQ3jK). Reports are handled confidentially.

## Enforcement

Organizers may take any action they deem appropriate, including warnings, disqualification, or removal from the venue, with no refund of anything and no appeal during the event.
